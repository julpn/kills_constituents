About this site

KillsConstituents.com is not affiliated with any political party or candidate. The site was developed by a small group of private citizens to hold members of Congress accountable for what their health care legislation will do to the people they represent in their districts. We've made our projections as accurate as possible, using the best expert analysis publicly available.

Methodology

Deaths: Based on the CBO report, Center for American Progress analysis, and a 2014 Annals of Internal Medicine study linking insurance rates to death rates.

Also an article from Vice summing up the "death rate" research.

Insurance loss:Based on analysis of the CBO report from the Center for American Progress.

Premium increase:Based on analysis from the AARP.
